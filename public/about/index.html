Tools that I use and recommend#

Obsidian - app for writing of everyday notes. Cost: free

iA Writer - for writing long texts. Cost: $30-$50 depending on the platform

Todoist - in my opinion, the best app for managing everyday to-do lists. Cost: free in basic option

DeepL - translator based on AI. Cost: free in basic option

Espanso - text expander with many options of customization. Cost: free

NordVPN - simply VPN. Cost: ~$60/year

Affinity - great software for graphic works, I use full package: Designer, Photo and Publisher. Cost: ~$200 for full package

Sendy (affiliate) - self-hosted app for sending newsletters (primarily with AmazonSES - you can send 10,000 emails for $1). Cost: $69 one time fee

n8n - absolutely great automation tool for building workflows. Cost: free in self-hosted version
